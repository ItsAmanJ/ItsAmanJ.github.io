About Me

Hey, I'm Aman 👋

I'm an 18-year-old student from the cultural heart of India — Varanasi — currently navigating the epic juggle of Class 12 CBSE boards and high-voltage JEE preparation.

My passion for building things with technology started early. Back in Class 10, I was already deep into Arduino and robotics — collaborating with a friend to build a human hand-following robot and presenting it to an audience. That project sparked something that never stopped: a drive to understand how hardware and software talk to each other.

Since then that curiosity has only expanded — from Android modding and running full Linux environments on a tablet, to dissecting finance and UPI ecosystems. I love pushing boundaries wherever I find them.

Outside the grind, you'll find me on the badminton court, diving into a Clash Royale match, editing cinematic clips, or vibing to Honey Singh.
